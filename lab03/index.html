Reflection: Lab 03

While completing Lab 3, I learned how linking an external stylesheet makes it much easier to control and update the look of my whole page at once. Moving my CSS out of the HTML helped me see the difference between content and design more clearly. Adding the second reflection paragraph also gave me a chance to compare what I’ve learned since Lab 2 and notice how my skills have grown. My coworkers were my main resource again, and they helped me troubleshoot small issues in my CSS along the way.
