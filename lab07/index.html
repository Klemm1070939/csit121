Lab 06 — Accessibility Evaluation: Colorado Avalanche

Screenshot of Site

1. What is the URL of the website?

https://www.nhl.com/avalanche/

2. What is the name of the website?

NHL Colorado Avalanche Official Website

3. Who is the site's target audience?

The site targets hockey fans, specifically fans of the Colorado Avalanche team

4. How is the site organized?

The site uses a top navigation bar with dropdown menus for News, Schedule, Team, Tickets, and Shop. The homepage displays featured stories, highlights, and team updates using a grid layout.

5. Which CRAP Design Principle does it use?

Contrast: The site uses a deep red and white theme that makes text and buttons easy to read. The red accent color draws attention to important things.

6. Accessibility Audit Score

Using the Accessibility Checker, the site received a score of 35%.

7. Site Effectiveness

The site effectively supports users in finding news, schedules, and tickets quickly. Navigation is straightforward and all key sections are easy to locate.

8. Site Efficiency

Page's content is grouped logically for quick access on computers and phones.

9. Engagement

The design is visually appealing, with large images, consistent branding.

10. Recommendation

The site could improve accessibility by increasing color contrast on text overlays and ensuring every image includes descriptive alt text for screen readers.
